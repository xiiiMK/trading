What is Trading?

Trading is the act of buying and selling financial instruments in various markets.

Definition: Trading involves the exchange of assets such as stocks, currencies, and commodities.

Types of Markets

  • Stock Market
  • Forex Market
  • Commodity Market
  • Crypto Market
Key Points: Each market has its own characteristics and trading hours.

Key Terms

Bid/Ask Price, Spread, Volume, etc.

Definition: The bid price is the highest price a buyer is willing to pay, while the ask price is the lowest price a seller will accept.
