Understanding Risk

Risk is an inherent part of trading, and understanding it is crucial for long-term success.

Definition: Risk refers to the potential for loss in trading and must be managed effectively.

Risk-Reward Ratio

Calculating the risk-reward ratio helps in making informed decisions about potential trades.

Key Points: A favorable risk-reward ratio is essential for successful trading strategies.

Setting Stop-Loss Orders

Stop-loss orders are essential for limiting potential losses and protecting your capital.

Definition: A stop-loss order automatically sells a security when it reaches a certain price.
