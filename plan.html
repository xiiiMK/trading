Components of a Trading Plan

A trading plan should include your goals, strategies, and risk management techniques.

Definition: A trading plan is a comprehensive outline that defines your trading strategy, including entry and exit points, risk management, and performance evaluation.

Setting Goals

Define clear and achievable trading goals to guide your actions and measure your success.

Key Points: Goals should be specific, measurable, attainable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).

Reviewing Your Plan

Regularly reviewing and adjusting your trading plan is essential for success in the ever-changing market.

Definition: A review process helps identify what works and what needs improvement in your trading strategy.
